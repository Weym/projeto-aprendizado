## Content
Network communication relies on four pillars:
1. **Sender**: The origin of the data.
2. **Receiver**: The destination.
3. **Medium**: The physical or logical path.
4. **Data**: The ~~information~~ data being transmitted.

Sender/Receiver can switch roles during communication.
