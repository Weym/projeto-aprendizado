## Content
**Semiotics** in networking refers to the study of signs and symbols used to communicate status between machines and humans.
* **Example:** Router LED lights (Red blinking = Loss of Signal/LOS; Green = Stable).
* **Example:** Wi-Fi signal bars (Visual representation of connection strength).
These signs allow users to quickly diagnose the state of the [network-communication-elements](network-communication-elements.md).

*can also refer to the process the data goes from being "randoms" 0s and 1s to data humans (or even the computer) can understand.
