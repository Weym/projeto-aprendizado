## Content
In a **Mesh Topology**, every device has a dedicated point-to-point connection with all (full mesh) or some (partial mesh) of the other devices in the network.
- **Pros**: Extremely high redundancy and reliability: ideal for load balancing.
- **Cons**: Complex and expensive.
- **Use case**: The Internet Backbones.
